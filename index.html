Town Information Application

Everything in that town, with just one app.

What is the “Town Information Application”?

The information application specialized for towns and housing complexes will maximize the attractiveness of the area and provide a rich experience for residents and visitors.

  • The latest information

    Efficiently obtain the latest information about stores, events and famous places

  • Function integretion

    Consolidates functions such as advertising, payments, reservations, and online shopping

  • Community revitalization

    Contribute to the revitalization of local communities and economies

Do you have those issues?

Issues of stores and companies

  • Name search is required to get information on SNS*

  • SNS has an information gap because the applications utilized differ by country and age group

  • Forgot to update SNS

Issues of locals

  • Difficult to keep up to date all latest information each time as areas are developed

  • A new store has opened in the previously vacant lot

  • Visiting the restaurant you wanted to go to, it is closed or temporarily closed

What you can do with the Application?

The Town Information application, which compiles real-time information on all stores, services, and facilities in a town or housing complex, makes local resident's life more convenient.

Stores,Restaurants and Companies

  • Revenue and attract customers from advertisement

    Advertisement allows you to attract customers and generate revenue (Monthly advertising fee is charged)
    → Management of the information you provide

  • Attract more customers and increase sales by posting on the application

    Store information, photos, SNS and coupons can be posted on the application (Usage fee is charged)
    → Increase in exposure attract more customers. It leads to higher sales

  • Revenue from the online store

    Orders can be taken from in and out of town through the online store (Fees charged as a percentage of sales costs)

  • Revenue from delivery

    Sales to a wide range of customers are available and the application enhance user convenience (A partial usage fee is charged for delivery costs)

Residents (Application users)

  • Life becomes more convenient

    Comfortable shopping and service are available

  • Easier payments

    One payment system can complete everything and improve the quality of life

  • Information can be viewed in one place

    Information is enriched by bringing all information together and getting the latest information

  • Improvement of well-being

    Enriched information and community revitalization will improve the well-being of its residents

Local, Municipalities, and Companies

  • Creation of new employment

    Increase in deliveries and other services will create jobs

  • Increase in immigrants and visitors

    Increase in immigrants and visitors
    → Recognition by more people will lead to local revitalization

  • Reduction of vacant lots

    As their lives in that area become more affluent, more people will move to the area and then vacant lots and tenants will decreased

  • Community revitalization

    The economy will be stimulated by a decrease in vacant lots and an increase in employment

Overall Benefits
